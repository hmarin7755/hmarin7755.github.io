Frequently Asked Questions

If you’ve ever wanted to resend an email to people who didn’t open it the first time, use Mailchimp’s mobile app to do it in just a few taps. When you resend an email campaign, it can sometimes cause unsubscribes, but if you only do it once, and within a day of the original send time, it may actually help you boost your engagement.

This app can do a lot for you when you're out and about, but it's not meant to replace the full version of Mailchimp. If you need to do something in your account that isn't available in the mobile app, access the full version of Mailchimp in a web browser.

Push notifications help inform you about new activity in your Mailchimp account. Enable push notifications in the mobile app to receive campaign and audience updates.
